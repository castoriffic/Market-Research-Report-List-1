<p><strong>The Terahertz Imaging Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Terahertz Imaging</li><li>Active Terahertz Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Terahertz imaging cameras are used for capturing and analyzing images in the terahertz frequency range, which lies between microwaves and infrared radiation. There are two main types of terahertz imaging cameras: passive terahertz imaging and active terahertz imaging. </p><p>Passive terahertz imaging cameras detect and analyze the naturally emitted terahertz radiation from objects. This type of camera is ideal for non-invasive imaging applications, such as security screening and medical imaging.</p><p>Active terahertz imaging cameras, on the other hand, emit their own terahertz radiation and then measure the reflected or transmitted signals. This allows for higher resolution imaging and is used in various applications including quality control, material characterization, and non-destructive testing.</p></p>
