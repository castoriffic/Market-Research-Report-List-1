<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle Traction Motors market is divided into two main types: Permanent Magnet Motor and AC Induction Motor. Permanent Magnet Motors utilize a permanent magnet to generate a magnetic field, resulting in high efficiency, compact size, and improved power density. On the other hand, AC Induction Motors operate on the principle of electromagnetic induction, using alternating current to induce magnetic fields and provide vehicle propulsion. Both these motor types play a crucial role in powering electric and hybrid vehicles, contributing to the advancement of the clean and sustainable transportation industry.</p></p>
