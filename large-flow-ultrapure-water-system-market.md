<p><p>The Large Flow Ultrapure Water System market is categorized into two types: Reverse Osmosis+Ion Exchange and Reverse Osmosis+EDI. In the Reverse Osmosis+Ion Exchange market, water is initially treated using reverse osmosis technology to remove impurities. It is then further purified using ion exchange to eliminate any remaining contaminants. Similarly, in the Reverse Osmosis+EDI market, water is first treated with reverse osmosis and then further purified using Electrodeionization (EDI) to achieve the highest level of purity. Both types of systems provide large-scale ultrapure water for various industrial applications.</p></p>
