Manufacturers are increasingly adopting recyclable and biodegradable variants of PVC foam boards to meet the demand for environmentally friendly products.</p><p>Overall, considering the continuous demand from the construction and advertising sectors, along with the adoption of sustainable materials, the PVC foam board market is expected to flourish in the coming years.</p></p>
