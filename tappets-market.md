<p><p>Tappets are components that help control valve motion in internal combustion engines. There are two main types of tappets in the market: flat tappets and roller tappets. Flat tappets have a flat contact surface and are commonly used in older engines. Roller tappets, on the other hand, have a cylindrical roller that reduces friction and wear, making them more efficient and reliable. While both types serve the same purpose, roller tappets are preferred in modern engines due to their improved performance and durability.</p></p>
