<p><p>The E-Bike Lights market can be categorized into two types, namely Front Lights and Rear Lights. Front Lights are installed on the front of the electric bikes and are designed to illuminate the path ahead during night rides. They ensure better visibility and safety for the rider. On the other hand, Rear Lights are mounted at the back of the e-bikes and help in making the bike visible to the vehicles coming from behind, thus minimizing the risk of accidents. Both front and rear lights play an important role in enhancing the overall safety of e-bike riders during low light conditions.</p></p>
