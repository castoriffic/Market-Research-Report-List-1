<p><p>The truck tachograph market consists of two main types: integrated and portable. Integrated tachographs are built into the truck's dashboard and provide real-time data on the vehicle's speed, distance traveled, and driving time. They are permanently installed and offer more accurate and reliable tracking. On the other hand, portable tachographs are devices that can be easily attached or detached from different vehicles. They offer flexibility and convenience for truckers who may need to switch between vehicles. Both types serve the purpose of monitoring and recording important data related to truck driving activities.</p></p>
