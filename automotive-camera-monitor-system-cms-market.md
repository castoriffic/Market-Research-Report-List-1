<p><p>The Automotive Camera Monitor System (CMS) is a visual aid system used in vehicles to provide real-time images of the surroundings to the driver. It typically consists of one or more cameras installed on the exterior of the vehicle that capture the images, and a monitor inside the vehicle that displays these images to the driver. This system helps in enhancing driver visibility, improving road safety, and reducing accidents by providing a better understanding of the surroundings, especially in blind spots and during parking.</p><p>The future outlook of the Automotive Camera Monitor System (CMS) market is highly promising. <p><p>The Automotive Camera Monitor System (CMS) market can be categorized into two types: Wireless Type and Wired Type. The Wireless Type utilizes wireless technology to transmit images from the camera to the monitor, eliminating the need for physical wiring. This type offers convenience and easy installation but may be affected by signal interference. On the other hand, the Wired Type requires physical connections between the camera and the monitor, ensuring stable and reliable video transmission. Although the installation process may be more complicated, this type is less susceptible to signal disruption.</p></p>
