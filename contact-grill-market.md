<p><p>The Contact Grill market offers two main types of grills: Liquid Propane (LP) grills and Natural Gas (NG) grills. LP grills are fueled by propane gas stored in tanks, making them portable and suitable for outdoor use. On the other hand, NG grills are connected directly to a gas line and rely on natural gas as fuel, making them a convenient option for outdoor or indoor use. Both grill types provide a quick and convenient cooking experience, with LP grills being more versatile and NG grills offering a continuous and uninterrupted fuel supply.</p></p>
