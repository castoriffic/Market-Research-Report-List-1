<p><p>Pillow Plate Heat Exchangers are available in two types: Single Embossed and Double Embossed. </p><p>The Single Embossed market refers to heat exchangers that have one side embossed with a pattern, while the other side remains smooth. This design allows for efficient heat transfer while maintaining a compact size and lightweight structure. </p><p>In contrast, Double Embossed heat exchangers have both sides embossed with patterns, resulting in enhanced heat transfer efficiency and increased overall performance. These heat exchangers are commonly used in industries that require high heating or cooling capacities.</p></p>
