<p><p>The electric power transmission transformers market includes three main types: low voltage transformers, medium voltage transformers, and high voltage transformers. Low voltage transformers are used to step down the voltage from the main supply to safely power appliances in residential and commercial settings. Medium voltage transformers are used for power distribution in industrial settings, converting power to a higher voltage for transmission to a larger area. High voltage transformers are utilized in substations to further step up the voltage for long-distance power transmission through transmission lines.</p></p>
