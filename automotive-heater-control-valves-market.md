<p><p>The automotive heater control valve market consists of two main types: electric and hydraulic valves. Electric control valves are operated electronically and regulate the flow of coolant through the heater core based on the temperature settings selected by the driver. Hydraulic control valves, on the other hand, rely on hydraulic pressure to regulate the flow of coolant. Both types of valves play a crucial role in maintaining the desired temperature inside the vehicle cabin, ensuring optimal comfort for the occupants.</p></p>
