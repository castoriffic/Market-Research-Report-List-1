<p><p>The mattress spring wire market consists of four types of springs: continuous coils, Bonnell coils, offset coils, and Marshall coils. Continuous coils are made from a single wire that forms a series of interconnected springs, providing durability and support. Bonnell coils have an hourglass shape, offering a bouncy feel and excellent support. Offset coils are similar to Bonnell but with a hinged design that allows for better contouring and pressure relief. Marshall coils are individually wrapped in fabric pockets, providing optimal comfort, contouring, and motion isolation.</p></p>
