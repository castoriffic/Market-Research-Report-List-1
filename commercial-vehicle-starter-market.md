<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Starter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Control</li><li>Electromagnetic Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Starter Market Types refer to different types of starter systems used in commercial vehicles. </p><p>1. Direct Control: In this type of starter, a solenoid is used to engage the starter with the engine flywheel. When the ignition key is turned, power is directly supplied to the solenoid, which then directly engages the starter motor and starts the engine.</p><p>2. Electromagnetic Control: In this type, an electromagnetic solenoid is used to engage the starter. When the ignition key is turned, power is supplied to the solenoid, creating a magnetic field that pulls a plunger, engaging the starter motor and starting the engine.</p></p>
