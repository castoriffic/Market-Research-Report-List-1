<p><p>Sanitary double seal valves are used in industries that require a high level of cleanliness and hygiene, such as food and pharmaceutical industries. There are two main types of these valves: single seat and double seat. Single seat valves have only one sealing surface between the plug and seat, offering a tight seal and preventing any backflow. On the other hand, double seat valves have two sealing surfaces, allowing for a greater flow capacity and providing additional safety against leakage. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining sanitary conditions during fluid control processes.</p></p>
