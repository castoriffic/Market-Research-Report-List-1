<p><p>A pneumatic belt sander is a power tool that uses compressed air to drive a sanding belt, which is used for sanding and smoothing surfaces. It consists of a motor, an air compressor, and a sanding belt that is wrapped around two rollers. Pneumatic belt sanders are commonly used in woodworking, metalworking, and automotive industries.</p><p>In terms of market analysis, the pneumatic belt sander market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand for pneumatic tools in various industries is driving the market growth. Pneumatic belt sanders are preferred over electric belt sanders due to their higher power output, durability, and ability to operate in harsh industrial conditions.</p><p>The market is also being driven by the rising adoption of automation in industrial processes, as pneumatic belt sanders are extensively used in automated manufacturing systems.
