<p><p>The walk-behind trenchers market consists of two types: wheel trenchers and chain trenchers. Wheel trenchers are equipped with a large wheel that rotates to dig trenches. They are suitable for softer soils and create shallow trenches. On the other hand, chain trenchers utilize a digging chain with teeth to cut through more challenging terrains. They are ideal for deeper and narrower trenches. Both types offer efficient and precise trenching solutions, catering to different soil types and trenching requirements.</p></p>
