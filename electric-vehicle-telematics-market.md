<p><p>Electric Vehicle Telematics market includes two main types: Plug and Play Telematics and Hardwired Install Telematics. Plug and Play Telematics refers to a system that can be easily installed and removed from the electric vehicle without any alterations. It offers flexibility, allowing users to switch between vehicles. On the other hand, Hardwired Install Telematics involves a permanent installation that requires wiring and integration into the vehicle's systems. This type offers a more permanent and secure solution, but it is less flexible in terms of moving between vehicles.</p></p>
