<p><p>Submarine repeaters are used to boost signals in undersea communication cables. In the market, there are three types based on the number of fiber pairs they support: 2 Fiber Pair, 4 Fiber Pair, and 8 Fiber Pair. A 2 Fiber Pair repeater can handle two separate data transmissions, a 4 Fiber Pair repeater can manage four, and an 8 Fiber Pair repeater can handle eight. These repeaters are integral in expanding the data capacity of submarine cables, allowing for higher-speed and more reliable international communications.</p></p>
