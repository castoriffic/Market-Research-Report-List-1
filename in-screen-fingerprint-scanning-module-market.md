<p><p>In the market for in-screen fingerprint scanning modules, there are two main types available - optical and ultrasonic. Optical fingerprint scanners use light to capture an image of the fingerprint and analyze it for identification. Ultrasonic fingerprint scanners, on the other hand, use sound waves to create a 3D image of the fingerprint, offering enhanced accuracy and security. These modules are integrated into smartphone screens, allowing users to conveniently unlock their devices by placing their finger on the designated area.</p></p>
