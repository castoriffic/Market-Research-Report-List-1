<p><p>The network office document solutions market consists of two main types: cloud-based DMS and on-premises DMS. Cloud-based DMS refers to document management systems that are hosted and accessed through the internet. It allows users to store, manage, and share documents online, without the need for physical infrastructure. On the other hand, on-premises DMS involves installing and managing the document management system within the organization's local network. This provides more control and security but requires the organization to maintain the necessary hardware and software on-site.</p></p>
