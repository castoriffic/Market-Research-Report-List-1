<p><p>Small size fans for cooling can be categorized into two main market types: axial fans and centrifugal fans. Axial fans are commonly used in applications requiring high airflow and low pressure, such as cooling electronic devices. These fans have blades that move air in a linear direction parallel to the fan shaft. On the other hand, centrifugal fans are suitable for applications with high-pressure requirements, such as cooling HVAC systems. These fans create airflow by blowing air perpendicular to the fan shaft, resulting in a higher pressure output.</p></p>
