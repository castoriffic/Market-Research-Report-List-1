<p><p>An Ultrasonic Wind Speed Direction Sensor is a device used to measure both the speed and direction of wind using ultrasonic technology. It consists of ultrasonic transducers that send and receive ultrasonic waves, and based on the time it takes for these waves to travel, it calculates the wind speed and direction. These sensors are widely used in various applications such as weather monitoring systems, wind farms, aviation, and maritime industries.</p><p>The Ultrasonic Wind Speed Direction Sensor Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
