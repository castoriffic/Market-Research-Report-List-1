<p><p>Polyimide tubing is a type of tubing made from a high-performance polymer known as polyimide. There are two main types of polyimide tubing in the market: seamless tubing and spiral wound tubing. Seamless tubing is manufactured without any visible seams, offering a smooth and continuous surface. On the other hand, spiral wound tubing is constructed by spirally winding a polyimide strip, resulting in a tube with visible ridges. Both types of tubing are used in various industries for their excellent resistance to high temperatures, chemicals, and abrasion.</p></p>
