<p><p>Division Multiplexer (DM) refers to a device used in telecommunications to combine multiple signals into a single transmission path. Frequency Division Multiplexer (FDM) is a type of DM that divides the available frequency spectrum into multiple non-overlapping channels, allowing simultaneous transmission of different signals. Wavelength Division Multiplexer (WDM) is another type of DM that operates on optical fibers, dividing the available wavelength spectrum into multiple channels. Both FDM and WDM enable efficient utilization of transmission media, increasing the capacity and flexibility of communication systems in different frequency or wavelength domains.</p></p>
