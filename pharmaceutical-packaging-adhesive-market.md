<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Packaging Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Adhesive</li><li>Solvent-free Adhesive</li><li>Waterborne Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pharmaceutical packaging adhesive market can be categorized into three types: solvent adhesive, solvent-free adhesive, and waterborne adhesives. Solvent adhesives are those that contain a volatile organic solvent as their primary component. Solvent-free adhesives, on the other hand, do not rely on solvents but instead utilize reactive monomers to form a bond. Waterborne adhesives are formulated with water as a carrier and provide an environmentally-friendly option. These adhesives are widely used in pharmaceutical packaging to securely seal containers and ensure product integrity.</p></p>
