<p><strong>The Automotive Power Window Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Master Switch</li><li>Auxiliary Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive power window switch market consists of two types: master switch and auxiliary switch. The master switch controls all the power windows in the vehicle and is usually located on the driver's side. It allows the driver to operate all the windows simultaneously. The auxiliary switch, on the other hand, is located on the individual doors and controls only the window on that specific door. It provides convenience to passengers by allowing them to control their own window without relying on the master switch.</p></p>
