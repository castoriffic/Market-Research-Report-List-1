Hard folding covers are made of solid materials and can be folded in multiple sections, allowing easy access to the truck bed. Soft rolling covers, on the other hand, are made of fabric and can be rolled up for quick access. Lastly, retractable covers are typically made of hard materials and can be retracted into a casing, offering full access to the truck bed when needed.</p></p>
