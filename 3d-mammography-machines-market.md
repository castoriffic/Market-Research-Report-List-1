<p><p>The 3D mammography machines market consists of different types of machines that are used to perform mammograms. Computed radiography machines utilize a cassette to capture the X-ray images, which are then processed digitally. Flat panel machines use a flat panel detector to directly convert X-rays into digital signals for image processing. CMOS flat machines use complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, which enhances image quality and reduces radiation exposure. These machines are important in detecting breast cancer and improving the accuracy of diagnosis.</p></p>
