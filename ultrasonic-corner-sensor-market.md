<p><p>The ultrasonic corner sensor market consists of three types: front corner sensor, back corner sensor, and side corner sensor. Front corner sensors are installed in the front of vehicles to detect obstacles during parking or maneuvering. Back corner sensors are commonly used in rear bumpers to aid drivers in reversing and prevent collisions. Side corner sensors are designed for the sides of vehicles to detect objects while changing lanes or parking. These types of sensors help enhance driver safety and provide added convenience in various driving scenarios.</p></p>
