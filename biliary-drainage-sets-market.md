<p><p>The biliary drainage sets market comprises two key types: biliary drainage catheters and biliary drainage bags. Biliary drainage catheters are flexible tubes inserted through a small incision into the bile duct to remove obstructive material or drain fluids from the liver. On the other hand, biliary drainage bags are used to collect the drained bile or fluids from the catheter. Both these devices are essential in managing obstructive biliary disorders, facilitating the bile flow, and providing relief to patients suffering from conditions such as jaundice or cholestasis.</p></p>
