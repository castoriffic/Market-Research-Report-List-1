<p><strong>The High Speed Trains Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wheel on Rail</li><li>Maglev</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Speed Trains market can be classified into two types: Wheel on Rail and Maglev. Wheel on Rail trains operate on conventional tracks, with wheels rolling on rails to provide traction and support. They are widely used across the world, offering high speeds and efficient transportation. On the other hand, Maglev trains use magnetic levitation technology, eliminating the need for wheels on tracks. They achieve high speeds by hovering above the guideway, utilizing magnetic forces for propulsion and levitation. Maglev trains are seen as a futuristic transportation solution and have limited deployment globally.</p></p>
