<p><p>The organic fertilizer market consists of three main types: plant-based, animal-based, and mineral-based fertilizers. Plant-based organic fertilizers are derived from plants and include materials like compost, manure, and crop residues. Animal-based organic fertilizers are derived from animal sources such as bone meal, blood meal, and fish emulsion. Mineral-based organic fertilizers are derived from natural mineral deposits and include materials like rock phosphate and kelp meal. These different types of organic fertilizers provide essential nutrients for plants, improve soil quality, and promote sustainable agricultural practices without the use of synthetic chemicals.</p></p>
