<p><p>Triethanolamine (TEA or TEOA) is an organic compound widely used as an intermediate and performance additive in various industries. TEA is mainly produced by the reaction of ammonia with ethylene oxide.</p><p>TEA is primarily used in applications such as surfactants, emulsifiers, corrosion inhibitors, cement grinding aids, and textile auxiliaries. It finds extensive use in cosmetics and personal care products, where it acts as a pH adjuster and emulsifier. In the pharmaceutical industry, TEA is used as an emulsifying agent and as a component in ointments and creams. It is also utilized in the manufacturing of agricultural chemicals, cleaning products, and metalworking fluids.</p><p>The global Triethanolamine (TEA or TEOA) market is witnessing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 12.1% during the forecast period.
