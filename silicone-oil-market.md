<p><p>Silicone oil is a highly stable and versatile non-flammable liquid polymer derived from silicone, a synthetic material made from silicon, oxygen, carbon, and hydrogen. <p><p>Silicone oil is classified into two main types: straight silicon oil and modified silicon oil. Straight silicone oil is a pure form of oil that is composed of straightforward silicone polymers and does not contain any additives or modifications. On the other hand, modified silicone oil refers to silicone oil that has been chemically modified or enhanced with additives to improve specific properties. These modifications can include changes in viscosity, surface tension, thermal stability, or other characteristics depending on the desired application or industry needs.</p></p>
