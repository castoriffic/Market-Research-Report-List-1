<p><p>Motor vehicle sensors are essential components that help monitor and control various aspects of a vehicle's functioning. Pressure sensors detect and measure pressure changes in the engine, fuel system, and tires. Temperature sensors monitor engine and coolant temperatures to prevent overheating. Speed sensors measure vehicle speed and help regulate functions like ABS and cruise control. Position sensors determine the location of various vehicle components and assist in navigation systems. O2 sensors monitor exhaust gases to ensure optimal fuel efficiency. Other sensors include those for detecting vehicle proximity, light levels, and tire pressure, among others.</p></p>
