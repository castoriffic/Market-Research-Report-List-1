<p><p>The high barrier type retort pouch market includes two main types: stand-up pouches and flat pouches. Stand-up pouches are designed to stand on their own and have a bottom gusset that allows them to retain their shape. They are ideal for products that need to be displayed vertically, such as soups or sauces. Flat pouches, on the other hand, are lay-flat bags without any gusset. They are commonly used for products that can be stored horizontally, like pet food or instant noodles. Both types offer excellent barrier properties, ensuring the long shelf life and preservation of the contents.</p></p>
