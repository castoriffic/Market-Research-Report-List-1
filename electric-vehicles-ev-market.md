<p><p>The electric vehicle (EV) market can be divided into three main types: Battery Electric Vehicles (BEV), Plug-in Electric Vehicles (PEV), and Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEV). BEVs are fully electric vehicles that run solely on electric power stored in batteries. PEVs are vehicles that can run on both electric power and gasoline, with the ability to be plugged in and charged. HEVs combine an internal combustion engine with an electric motor, utilizing both gasoline and electricity for propulsion. These types cater to different consumer needs and preferences in the growing market of electric vehicles.</p></p>
