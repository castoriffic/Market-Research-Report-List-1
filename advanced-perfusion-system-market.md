<p><p>The advanced perfusion system market includes two main types: single roller pump heart-lung machine and double roller pump heart-lung machine. The single roller pump heart-lung machine is a device used during cardiac surgeries to pump oxygenated blood throughout the body. It contains a single roller pump that helps maintain blood circulation. On the other hand, the double roller pump heart-lung machine utilizes two roller pumps for enhanced perfusion support. These systems enable precise control and monitoring of blood flow, oxygenation, and temperature during complex heart surgeries.</p></p>
