<p><p>Thermal laminating films are used for laminating various materials to add durability and protection. BOPP-based thermal lamination films are made from bi-axially oriented polypropylene, known for its excellent clarity and resistance to moisture. BOPET-based films are produced from bi-axially oriented polyester, offering high tensile strength and superior chemical resistance. BOPA-based thermal lamination films are derived from bi-axially oriented polyamide, providing exceptional oxygen and aroma barrier properties. These different types cater to diverse requirements, ensuring optimal protection and enhancement of the laminated materials.</p></p>
