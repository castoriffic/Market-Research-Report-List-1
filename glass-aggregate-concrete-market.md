<p><p>The glass aggregate concrete market consists of three types: clear glass chip, mirror glass chip, and colored glass chip. Clear glass chip is made from transparent glass and is commonly used in flooring and road construction. Mirror glass chip is created by crushing reflective glass and is mainly used in decorative applications such as countertops and walls. Colored glass chip is produced by crushing tinted glass and is utilized for decorative purposes in features like terrazzo flooring and landscaping. These variations offer diverse aesthetic options for incorporating glass into concrete projects.</p></p>
