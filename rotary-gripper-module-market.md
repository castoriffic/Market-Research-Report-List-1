<p><p>The rotary gripper module market includes two main types: parallel and angular. In the parallel market, the gripper module moves in a straight line, parallel to the object being gripped. This type is commonly used for applications that require high precision and speed, such as assembly lines. On the other hand, the angular market involves rotary gripper modules that move in a rotational manner. This type is suitable for tasks that involve rotating or manipulating objects, such as packaging or sorting operations. Both types cater to specific industrial needs, providing efficient and reliable gripping solutions.</p></p>
