<p><p>Flaxseed oil is extracted using two main methods: squeezing and leaching. The squeezing method involves pressing the seeds to extract the oil, typically resulting in a more natural, unrefined product. This method preserves the nutrients and flavor of the oil. On the other hand, the leaching method involves using chemicals or solvents to separate the oil from the seeds. This method produces a more refined oil, but it may also strip away some of the beneficial compounds and flavors found in the natural oil.</p></p>
