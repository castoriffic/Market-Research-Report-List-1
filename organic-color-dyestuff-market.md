<p><p>Organic color dyestuffs are classified into four main types: Azoic Dyestuff, Phthalocyanine Dyestuff, High-performance Dyestuff, and Others. Azoic dyestuffs are known for their versatility and are widely used in textile and leather industries. Phthalocyanine dyestuffs, on the other hand, are highly stable and have excellent colorfastness, making them suitable for applications such as printing inks and plastics. High-performance dyestuffs offer enhanced color intensity and superior lightfastness, making them ideal for industries like paints and coatings. The "Others" category includes various organic color dyestuffs used in niche applications, such as fluorescent dyes and pigment dispersions.</p></p>
