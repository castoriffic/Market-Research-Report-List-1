The Combiner HUD projects information onto a separate combiner located in the driver's line of sight, allowing them to view information without looking away from the road. On the other hand, the Windshield HUD directly projects information onto the windshield itself, enabling a more seamless integration of information in the driver's field of vision. These two types cater to different preferences and technological requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
