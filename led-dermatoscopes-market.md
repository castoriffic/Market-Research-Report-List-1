<p><p>LED dermatoscopes are advanced medical devices used for examining the skin. Contact dermatoscopes require direct contact with the skin to analyze and capture magnified images. They are often handheld and provide detailed images for accurate diagnosis. On the other hand, noncontact dermatoscopes use a lens-system to capture images without touching the skin, making them suitable for sensitive areas. Both types of dermatoscopes utilize LED lights for illumination, offering improved image quality and enhancing the accuracy of skin examinations.</p></p>
