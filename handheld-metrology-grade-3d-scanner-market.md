<p><p>The handheld metrology grade 3D scanner market consists of two main types: laser and structure light scanners. Laser scanners use laser beams to measure distances and capture precise details, making them suitable for scanning complex objects with high accuracy. Structure light scanners project patterns onto the object and analyze the deformation of these patterns to create a 3D model. They are ideal for scanning smaller objects that require less detailed measurements. Both types of scanners offer efficient and portable solutions for industrial and research applications.</p></p>
