<p><p>Centrifugal draught fans are used in various industrial applications for ventilation and air movement. There are three main types: radial, backward, and forward. Radial fans have blades that are curved backwards, providing a high-pressure output. Backward fans have blades that are curved in the opposite direction, providing a medium-pressure output. Forward fans have blades that are curved in the same direction as the fan rotation, producing a low-pressure output. These different types of fans are designed to meet specific requirements in terms of pressure, volume, and efficiency for different applications.</p></p>
