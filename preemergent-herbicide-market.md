<p><p>The preemergent herbicide market consists of two main types: selective herbicides and non-selective herbicides. Selective herbicides are designed to target specific types of weeds while leaving the desired plants unharmed. They are commonly used in lawns, gardens, and agricultural fields to control the growth of unwanted grasses or broadleaf weeds. On the other hand, non-selective herbicides are intended to eliminate all vegetation they come into contact with, making them suitable for driveways, sidewalks, or areas where complete weed eradication is desired. The choice between the two depends on the specific vegetation management needs of the user.</p></p>
