<p><p>Automotive acoustic glass refers to specialized glass used in vehicles to reduce noise and provide a quieter interior. Double deck glass involves the use of two glass layers with a vacuum or gas-filled space between them, enhancing sound insulation and reducing noise levels. Three layers of glass, on the other hand, comprise two outer layers of glass with an inner acoustic interlayer, effectively dampening sound vibrations. Both types offer improved acoustic performance, ensuring a more comfortable and enjoyable driving experience by minimizing external noise penetration into the vehicle cabin.</p></p>
