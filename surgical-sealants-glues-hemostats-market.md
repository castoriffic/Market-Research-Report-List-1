<p><p>The surgical sealants, glues, and hemostats market includes three different types of products that are used in the field of medicine. Sealants are substances applied to close wounds or incisions, ensuring a secure and leak-proof closure. Medical glues are adhesive substances used to bond tissues together, eliminating the need for sutures or staples. Hemostats, on the other hand, are used to control bleeding during surgery by promoting clotting. These products are vital in ensuring effective wound closure, minimizing blood loss, and improving patient outcomes in surgical procedures.</p></p>
