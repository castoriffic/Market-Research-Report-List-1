Glass-filled PEEK contains glass fibers, enhancing its strength, stiffness, and dimensional stability. Carbon-filled PEEK includes carbon fibers, offering exceptional mechanical properties and resistance to wear and fatigue. Un-filled PEEK is reinforced neither with glass nor carbon fibers, providing excellent electrical and chemical resistance. These different market types cater to diverse applications, with glass-filled PEEK being suitable for aerospace and automotive industries, carbon-filled PEEK for medical and engineering applications, and un-filled PEEK for electrical and electronic components.</p></p>
