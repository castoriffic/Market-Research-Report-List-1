<p><p>The EV bus market consists of two main types: Battery Electric Bus (BEB) and Hybrid Bus. Battery Electric Buses are fully powered by rechargeable batteries, offering zero-emission transportation. They do not rely on any fossil fuels and require charging infrastructure. On the other hand, Hybrid Buses combine both electric and conventional engine systems. They have smaller batteries and use regenerative braking to charge them. The conventional engine helps increase range, making hybrid buses a transition option for cities not yet ready for fully electric transportation.</p></p>
