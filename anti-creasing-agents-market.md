<p><p>The Anti-Creasing Agents market includes various types such as Dye-Bath Lubricant/Anti-Creasing Agent and Wet Processing Lubricant/Anti-Creasing Agent. The Dye-Bath Lubricant/Anti-Creasing Agent is used to prevent fabric creasing during the dyeing process. It acts as a lubricant between fabrics and dye materials, reducing friction and ensuring even color absorption. On the other hand, the Wet Processing Lubricant/Anti-Creasing Agent is employed in wet processing operations like washing, bleaching, and mercerizing. It provides lubrication and reduces creasing during these processes, enhancing the fabric's appearance and durability.</p></p>
