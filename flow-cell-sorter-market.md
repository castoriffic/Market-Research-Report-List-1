<p><p>Flow cell sorters are advanced scientific instruments used for sorting and analyzing cells in biological research. The fluorescent-activated flow cell sorter employs fluorescent labels to identify and separate cells based on their specific characteristics. It enables researchers to study and isolate specific cell populations in complex samples. On the other hand, the magnetic-activated flow cell sorter uses magnetic beads bound to specific cell markers to isolate and separate cells of interest. Both types of sorters provide valuable tools for various applications in medical and biological research.</p></p>
