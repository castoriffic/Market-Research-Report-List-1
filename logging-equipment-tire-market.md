<p><p>The logging equipment tire market encompasses three main types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane. Pneumatic tires are filled with compressed air and provide better shock absorption and traction, making them suitable for rough terrains. Solid tires are made of solid rubber and are puncture-resistant, ideal for environments with debris or sharp objects. Polyurethane tires combine the durability of solid tires with the cushioning of pneumatic tires, offering a balance between performance and comfort. Each type caters to specific logging requirements, allowing loggers to choose based on their needs and terrain conditions.</p></p>
