<p><p>The semiconductor tempering furnace market comprises various types of furnaces used in the semiconductor manufacturing process. Vertical furnaces are designed with a vertical chamber orientation, allowing for high throughput and uniform processing. Horizontal furnaces, on the other hand, have a horizontal chamber orientation and are suitable for larger wafer sizes. Other types of furnaces include rapid thermal processing (RTP) and diffusion furnaces. RTP furnaces provide quick heating and cooling cycles, while diffusion furnaces are used for depositing thin films onto semiconductor wafers.</p></p>
