<p><p>The COVID-19 molecular diagnostics market includes three major types. First, high-throughput sequencing analyzes the genetic material of the virus to detect and identify COVID-19. Second, nucleic acid detection based on real-time fluorescent quantitative PCR uses fluorescent tags to measure the presence and quantity of the virus's genetic material. Third, immunological detection involves testing for antibodies or antigens produced in response to COVID-19 infection. These different types of molecular diagnostics help in accurate and timely identification of COVID-19 cases for effective disease management and control.</p></p>
