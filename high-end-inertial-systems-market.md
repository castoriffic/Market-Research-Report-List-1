<p><p>The high-end inertial systems market consists of three main types: high-end inertial measurement units (IMUs), high-end accelerometers, and high-end gyroscopes. High-end IMUs are advanced devices used to capture and measure an object's acceleration, angular velocity, and magnetic field. High-end accelerometers are sensors that detect changes in velocity and acceleration. High-end gyroscopes measure an object's rotational speed or angular velocity. These high-quality systems are utilized in various applications such as navigation, aerospace, defense, and industrial sectors, where precision and accuracy are critical.</p></p>
