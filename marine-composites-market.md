<p><p>The marine composites market includes various types of materials used in the marine industry. One type is Glass Fiber Reinforced Plastics (GFRPs), which are composed of glass fibers embedded in a polymer matrix. Polymer Fiber Reinforced Plastics (PFRPs) consist of other types of fibers, such as aramid or carbon, combined with a polymer matrix. Carbon Fiber Reinforced Plastics (CFRPs) are made up of carbon fibers and a polymer matrix. Lastly, the "Others" category includes composites made from materials like natural fibers or hybrid composites.</p></p>
