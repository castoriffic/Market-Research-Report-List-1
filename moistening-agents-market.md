They also help in preventing crystallization and maintaining the moisture content in baked goods, candies, and confectionery items. In the pharmaceutical industry, moistening agents are used to improve the viscosity and stability of drugs and prevent their degradation.</p><p>In the personal care and cosmetics industry, moistening agents are used in skincare products, lotions, and creams to provide hydration and maintain the moisture balance of the skin.
