<p><p>Automotive radar modules operate using radio frequency signals to detect and measure the distance between vehicles on the road. These modules are categorized into two types: 77 GHz and 79 GHz. The 77 GHz market primarily includes short-range applications for advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS), providing features like blind-spot detection and collision avoidance.
