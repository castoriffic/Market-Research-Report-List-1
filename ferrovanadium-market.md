It is available in different market types, such as FeV40, FeV50, FeV60, and FeV80, which signify the percentage of vanadium present in the alloy. FeV40 consists of 40% vanadium, FeV50 contains 50% vanadium, FeV60 has 60% vanadium, and FeV80 is composed of 80% vanadium. Additionally, there is a market for Nitrided Ferrovanadium, which involves the addition of nitrogen to further augment the alloy's properties. These different market types cater to diverse industry needs and applications.</p></p>
