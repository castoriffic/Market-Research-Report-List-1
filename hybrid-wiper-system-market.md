<p><p>Hybrid Wiper System market comprises three main types: Wiper Motor, Windshield Wiper, and Rain Sensor. The Wiper Motor type includes the mechanism responsible for driving the wiper arms back and forth across the windshield. Windshield Wiper type refers to the physical wiper blades that clear the windshield. Lastly, the Rain Sensor type involves a sensor that detects rain and automatically activates the wipers. These components work together to create an effective and efficient wiper system for vehicles, improving visibility during rainy conditions.</p></p>
