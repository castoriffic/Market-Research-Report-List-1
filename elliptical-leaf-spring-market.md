<p><p>The elliptical leaf spring market consists of two main types: multi-leaf spring and mono-leaf spring. Multi-leaf springs are made up of stacked curved strips of metal, providing greater load-bearing capacity and stability. They are commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles and trucks. On the other hand, mono-leaf springs are single strips of metal with a parabolic shape, offering a smoother ride and suitable for lighter vehicles like cars and SUVs. Both types of leaf springs play important roles in the automotive industry, catering to different vehicle types and load requirements.</p></p>
