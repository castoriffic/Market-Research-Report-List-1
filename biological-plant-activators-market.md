<p><p>Biological plant activators are used to enhance plant growth and protection against diseases and pests. The market includes different types of products such as water-dispersible granules, water-soluble granules, solutions, and wettable powders. Water-dispersible granules are formulated to mix with water before application, while water-soluble granules dissolve completely in water. Solutions are ready-to-use formulations that can be directly applied to plants. Wettable powders are finely ground solids that can be suspended in water. These different types provide options for farmers and growers to choose the most suitable form for their needs.</p></p>
