<p><strong>The Car Chassis Dyno Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Roller Type</li><li>Multi Roller Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Car chassis dyno market includes two main types: single roller type and multi-roller type. A single roller type chassis dyno consists of a single roller that is connected to the vehicle's drive wheels. It measures the vehicle's horsepower and torque output by simulating road conditions. On the other hand, a multi-roller type chassis dyno uses multiple rollers to support each individual tire of the vehicle, providing more accurate and precise measurements of power and torque. These dynos are used by automotive enthusiasts, performance shops, and manufacturers to measure and optimize a vehicle's performance.</p></p>
