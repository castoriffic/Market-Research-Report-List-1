<p><p>The Road Dredging Vehicle market comprises two main types: Trailed Type and Self-propelled Type. Trailed Type vehicles are designed to be towed by another vehicle. They are usually smaller in size and have lower capacity. On the other hand, Self-propelled Type vehicles are equipped with their own propulsion system, enabling them to move independently. These vehicles are commonly larger in size and have higher capacity in order to handle larger dredging tasks.
