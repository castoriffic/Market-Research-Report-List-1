The market for SBCs can be categorized into three types: styrene butadiene styrene (SBS), styrene isoprene styrene (SIS), and hydrogenated styrenic block copolymer (HSBC). SBS offers excellent elasticity and is commonly used in the footwear and adhesive industries. SIS provides improved tackiness and is used in the pressure-sensitive adhesive sector. HSBC, created through hydrogenation, offers enhanced thermal and chemical resistance, making it suitable for applications requiring durability and stability.</p></p>
