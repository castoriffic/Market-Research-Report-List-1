<p><p>Medical Audiological Devices Market Types can be classified into various categories. Behind-the-ear hearing aids (BTE) are worn behind the ear, offering comfort and ease of use. Receiver in the canal hearing aids (RITE) are discreet and provide excellent sound quality due to the receiver being placed in the ear canal. In the canal hearing aids (IIC) are almost invisible as they fit entirely inside the ear canal. Completely in the canal hearing aids (CIC) are similar but slightly more visible. Body-worn hearing aids are worn on the body and connected to the ear via a wire. There are also other types available in the market.</p></p>
