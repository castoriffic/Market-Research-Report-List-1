Hollow glass materials have a low density due to the presence of air-filled cavities, making them suitable for various industries such as marine and aerospace. Light composites refer to materials that are lightweight and have excellent buoyancy properties, making them ideal for applications where weight reduction and high strength are crucial.</p></p>
