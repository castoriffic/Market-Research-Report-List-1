<p><p>The Dental Extraoral X-Rays Market includes two types: Panoramic Type and CBCT Type. On the other hand, CBCT (Cone Beam Computed Tomography) X-rays provide three-dimensional images, allowing dentists to assess specific areas in greater detail, such as dental implant planning. Both types of X-rays are valuable tools in dentistry, enabling dentists to diagnose and plan treatments accurately, resulting in enhanced patient care and outcomes.</p></p>
