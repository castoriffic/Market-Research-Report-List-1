<p><ul><li>Closed Type</li><li>Partially Closed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Sound-proof Cabinets market consists of two main types: Closed Type and Partially Closed Type. Closed Type cabinets are completely sealed units that provide maximum sound insulation. They are ideal for environments requiring complete silence, such as recording studios or scientific laboratories. Partially Closed Type cabinets, on the other hand, have small openings or vents to allow for air circulation while still reducing external noise. These cabinets are commonly used in offices or commercial spaces where some noise reduction is needed, but not complete silence.</p></p>
