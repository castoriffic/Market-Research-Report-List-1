Speech Mapping involves assessing the amplification of specific speech sounds to ensure optimal hearing aid performance. Acoustic Testing involves measuring the hearing aid's response to different sound environments. Programming involves adjusting the hearing aid's settings to meet the individual's specific hearing needs. These market types aim to provide effective and personalized solutions for individuals with hearing impairments.</p></p>
