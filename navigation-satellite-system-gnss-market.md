It includes global constellations like GPS (US), GLONASS (Russia), Galileo (European Union), and BeiDou (China), which collectively cover the entire globe. Regional constellations, such as IRNSS (India) and QZSS (Japan), focus on specific regions. Satellite-Based Augmentations improve the accuracy and reliability of GNSS by using additional ground-based stations to enhance the positioning data. These technologies play a crucial role in various industries like transportation, agriculture, and telecommunications.</p></p>
