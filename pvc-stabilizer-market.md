<p><p>The PVC stabilizer market comprises four types of stabilizers: lead stabilizers, mixed metal stabilizers, tin stabilizers, and organic stabilizers. Lead stabilizers are commonly used due to their cost-effectiveness and high heat stability. Mixed metal stabilizers are a blend of various metals and are favored for their excellent weatherability. Tin stabilizers offer good clarity and are often used in transparent applications. Organic stabilizers, made from organic compounds, are eco-friendly and widely used due to their low toxicity. These stabilizers help maintain the stability and performance of PVC materials.</p></p>
