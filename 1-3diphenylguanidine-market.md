<p><p>1,3-Diphenylguanidine (DPG) is a white to pale pink crystal powder that is primarily used as an accelerator in the rubber industry. It belongs to the guanidine family and is widely utilized as a secondary accelerator in sulfur cured latex, natural and synthetic rubber compounds. DPG promotes the cross-linking reaction, which improves the hardness and elasticity of rubber products, making it an important ingredient in the manufacturing of products such as tires, belts, hoses, footwear, and rubber gloves.</p><p>The 1,3-Diphenylguanidine Market is expected to witness steady growth, with a projected CAGR of 6.2% during the forecast period.
