<p><p>The steering compasses market consists of two main types: magnetic and electronic. Magnetic compasses rely on the Earth's magnetic field to provide direction, using a magnetized needle that aligns with the magnetic poles. On the other hand, electronic compasses use sensors, such as magnetometers, accelerometers, and gyroscopes, to determine direction based on factors like motion and orientation. While magnetic compasses are traditional and reliable, electronic compasses offer additional features such as digital displays and greater accuracy. Both types cater to the navigation needs of different individuals and industries.</p></p>
