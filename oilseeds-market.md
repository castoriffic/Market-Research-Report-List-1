<p><p>Oilseeds refer to the seeds of various plants that are primarily used for extracting oil. Common oilseeds include soybeans, sunflower seeds, rapeseeds, and palm kernels. The increasing global population, coupled with rising disposable incomes, is expected to drive the demand for vegetable oils, which will, in turn, boost the demand for oilseeds.
