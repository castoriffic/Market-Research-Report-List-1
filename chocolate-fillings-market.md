<p><p>Chocolate fillings refer to the various types of delectable mixtures used to fill chocolate products. Milk fillings usually contain condensed or powdered milk for a creamy and smooth experience. Fruits fillings involve fruit purees or jams, contributing a fruity taste and enhancing the chocolate's sweetness. Lastly, other market types include unique fillings such as liqueurs, coffee, or even savory flavors like cheese or bacon.</p></p>
