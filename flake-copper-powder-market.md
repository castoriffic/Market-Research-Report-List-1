<p><p>The flake copper powder market is segmented into four categories based on particle size: Below 20 μm, 20~40 μm, 40~80 μm, and 80~120 μm. Below 20 μm copper powder particles are very fine and ideal for applications requiring high surface area and good conductivity. 20~40 μm particles are larger and suitable for applications like conductive films. 40~80 μm particles have moderate size and are used in powder metallurgy and diamond tools. Lastly, 80~120 μm particles are bigger and commonly employed in brazing and metal injection molding processes.</p></p>
