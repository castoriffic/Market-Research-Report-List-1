The neutralization method involves reacting tungsten-containing materials with ammonium hydroxide, leading to the formation of APT. The tungsten acid method involves treating tungsten trioxide or tungstic acid with ammonia solution to produce APT. Lastly, the thermal decomposition method involves heating tungsten-containing ammonium salts to a specific temperature to obtain APT.
