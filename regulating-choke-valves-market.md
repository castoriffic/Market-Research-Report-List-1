<p><p>Regulating choke valves are used in industries to control fluid flow. The needle and seat type choke valve features a needle-shaped stem that fits into a seat, allowing precise flow control. Multiple orifice valves have multiple small holes for flow regulation and are suitable for high-pressure applications. Cage valves consist of a cage-like structure that encloses multiple orifices, allowing accurate flow modulation. These market types cater to different needs, providing options for industries to choose the most appropriate choke valve for their specific fluid control requirements.</p></p>
