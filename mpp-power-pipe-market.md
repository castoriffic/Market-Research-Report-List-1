<p><p>The MPP Power Pipe market consists of two main types: Excavation Type and Non-excavation Type. Excavation Type involves digging trenches to install power pipes underground. This method is typically used in areas where the ground is easily accessible and does not have any obstructions. On the other hand, Non-excavation Type involves installing power pipes without the need for digging trenches, using techniques like horizontal directional drilling. This method is preferred in areas where there are obstacles or where minimum disruption to the surface is desired.</p></p>
