<p><p>The Inline Automated X-ray Inspection (AXI) market consists of two types: 2D AXI and 3D AXI. 2D AXI involves the inspection of electronic components using X-ray technology to identify defects such as soldering issues, component misalignment, or missing components. It provides a two-dimensional view of the components. On the other hand, 3D AXI employs advanced X-ray techniques to generate three-dimensional images, enabling more accurate and detailed inspection of complex circuit boards. Both 2D and 3D AXI technologies play a critical role in ensuring the quality and reliability of electronic devices.</p></p>
