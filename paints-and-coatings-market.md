<p><ul><li>Water-Based Paint</li><li>Solvent-Based Paint</li><li>Powder Paint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paints and coatings market can be divided into three main types: water-based paint, solvent-based paint, and powder paint. Water-based paint is made by mixing pigments with water and offers low odor and easy cleanup. Solvent-based paint is created by mixing pigments with organic solvents and provides excellent durability and resistance. Powder paint is produced by applying heat to a mixture of pigments and resins, resulting in a tough and attractive finish. All three types have their unique characteristics and are used for various applications in industries such as construction, automotive, and furniture.</p></p>
