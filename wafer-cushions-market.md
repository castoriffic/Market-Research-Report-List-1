<p><ul><li>Conductive Polyethylene Cushions</li><li>Anti Static Polyurethane Cushions</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer cushions market includes various types of cushions, such as conductive polyethylene cushions and anti-static polyurethane cushions. Conductive polyethylene cushions are designed to provide protection against static electricity by effectively grounding any electrostatic charges. On the other hand, anti-static polyurethane cushions are specifically engineered to dissipate static charges, preventing them from building up and causing damage. Both types of cushions play a vital role in the wafer industry by safeguarding sensitive wafers from potential electrostatic discharge-related issues.</p></p>
