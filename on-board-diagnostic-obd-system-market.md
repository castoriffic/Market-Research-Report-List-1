<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The On-board Diagnostic (OBD) System market consists of two main types: Threshold Monitoring and Comprehensive Component Monitoring. Threshold Monitoring involves the monitoring of specific thresholds such as emission levels or engine performance, where the OBD system alerts the driver if these thresholds are exceeded. Comprehensive Component Monitoring, on the other hand, involves monitoring and diagnosing various components of the vehicle, such as the engine, transmission, and brakes, to detect any malfunctions or issues. Both types of OBD systems play a crucial role in ensuring the optimal performance and efficiency of vehicles.</p></p>
