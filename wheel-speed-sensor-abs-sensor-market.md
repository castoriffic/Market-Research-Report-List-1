<p><p>A Wheel Speed Sensor, also known as an ABS Sensor, is a crucial component of the Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) in vehicles. Its main function is to monitor the rotational speed of the wheels and send this information to the ABS control module. This enables the system to prevent the wheels from locking up during heavy braking, thus ensuring stability and control of the vehicle.</p><p>The Wheel Speed Sensor (ABS Sensor) Market is experiencing significant growth due to several factors. Firstly, the increasing adoption of ABS systems in both passenger cars and commercial vehicles is driving the demand for wheel speed sensors. <p><p>The Wheel Speed Sensor (ABS Sensor) market includes two main types: Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type. The Hall Type sensor operates by detecting the changes in a magnetic field and converting them into electrical signals. On the other hand, the Magnetic Electric Type sensor uses a magnetic ring and coil to generate an electric current when the wheel rotates. Both types are crucial for anti-lock braking systems, as they provide essential data on wheel speed to ensure effective braking and vehicle stability.</p></p>
