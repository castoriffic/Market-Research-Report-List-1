In the market with rubber dome, a layer of rubber is placed under the membrane to provide a tactile feel when pressed. This type is commonly used in keyboards and remote controls. On the other hand, the market without rubber dome relies solely on the membrane layer for touch activation. This type is often found in applications requiring a sleek and flat design, like touch screens and control panels.</p></p>
