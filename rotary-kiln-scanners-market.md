Stereo scanners, on the other hand, utilize multiple sensors placed at various locations to provide a more comprehensive and accurate measurement of temperature distribution within the kiln. These scanners are essential in maintaining optimal operation and efficiency of the rotary kiln, ensuring smooth processing of materials.</p></p>
