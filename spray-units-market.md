<p><p>Spray units are categorized into low pressure and high pressure markets based on their operating capabilities. Low pressure spray units operate at lower pressure levels, typically less than 1000 psi, and are suitable for applications such as gardening, lawn care, and insect control. High pressure spray units, on the other hand, operate at higher pressure levels, typically ranging from 1000 to 3000 psi, and are used for more demanding tasks like industrial cleaning, paint spraying, and agricultural spraying. The distinction between low pressure and high pressure units allows customers to choose the appropriate spray unit for their specific needs.</p></p>
