<p><p>The electric intercity bus market consists of three main types: battery electric buses, plug-in hybrid buses, and hybrid buses. Battery electric buses are fully powered by electricity and do not use any fossil fuels. Plug-in hybrid buses have both an electric motor and an internal combustion engine, allowing them to switch between electric and conventional power. Hybrid buses combine an electric motor with a smaller internal combustion engine, utilizing regenerative braking to recharge the battery. These buses provide eco-friendly solutions in the intercity transportation sector by reducing emissions and promoting sustainability.</p></p>
