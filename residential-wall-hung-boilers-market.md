<p><p>The residential wall-hung boilers market is divided into two main types: regular wall-hung boilers and condens wall-hung boilers. Regular wall-hung boilers are traditional boilers that heat water and distribute it throughout the home for various uses. On the other hand, condens wall-hung boilers are more advanced and efficient, as they not only heat water but also harness the steam produced during the heating process to generate additional heat. This makes condens wall-hung boilers more energy-efficient and environmentally friendly options for residential heating.</p></p>
