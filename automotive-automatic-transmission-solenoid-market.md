<p><ul><li>Variable Force Solenoid</li><li>On-Off Solenoid</li><li>Pulse-Width Modulated Solenoid</li><li>Low Leak Variable Bleed Solenoid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive automatic transmission solenoid market includes various types of solenoids. Variable force solenoids are designed to offer variable force output to control pressure. On-off solenoids are used to open or close fluid channels. Pulse-width modulated solenoids control pressure by varying the pulse width of the signal. Low leak variable bleed solenoids allow small, controlled amounts of fluid leakage. These different types of solenoids serve specific purposes in controlling fluid pressure and flow within automatic transmissions, ensuring smooth and efficient operation of the vehicle.</p></p>
