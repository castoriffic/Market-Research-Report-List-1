<p><p>Profiled fibre is available in various market types including triangles, polygonal, flat ribbon shape, hollow, and others. Triangular profiled fibres have a unique triangular shape, providing increased surface area for better adhesion and dispersion in concrete applications. Polygonal profiled fibres have multiple sides, enhancing their mechanical anchorage within the matrix. Flat ribbon shape profiled fibres have a long, flat appearance, enabling improved tensile and flexural strength. Hollow profiled fibres are characterized by their empty center, optimizing their lightweight and insulating properties. Lastly, other market types encompass additional profile shapes specifically designed to meet various performance requirements in different applications.</p></p>
