<p><p>The plastic fencing market consists of various types, including vinyl, HDPE (high-density polyethylene), wood-plastic composites (WPC), polystyrene, and polyethylene. Vinyl fencing is made of PVC (polyvinyl chloride), known for its durability and low maintenance. HDPE fencing is crafted from high-density polyethylene, providing strength and resistance to chemicals. WPC fencing combines wood fibers and plastic, offering a natural appearance with durability. Polystyrene fencing utilizes a lightweight material known for insulation properties. Polyethylene fencing is manufactured from a versatile plastic known for its flexibility and strength in outdoor applications.</p></p>
