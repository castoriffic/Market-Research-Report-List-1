<p><p>The automotive lighting market comprises three main types: halogen lighting, HID lighting, and LED lighting. Halogen lighting is the most common and least expensive option, providing a yellowish light with moderate brightness. HID lighting, also known as xenon lighting, offers a brighter and bluer light than halogen, with longer durability. LED lighting is the most advanced and energy-efficient option, providing brighter and crisper light, longer lifespan, and lower power consumption. LED lighting is gradually gaining popularity due to its superior performance and eco-friendly features in the automotive industry.</p></p>
