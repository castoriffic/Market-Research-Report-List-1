Aluminum FRP offers a viable solution due to its unique combination of strength and low weight.</p><p>Additionally, increasing investment in infrastructure development, particularly in emerging economies, is further propelling the demand for Aluminum FRP in the construction industry. The material's corrosion resistance and durability make it suitable for applications such as bridges, pipelines, and buildings.</p><p>Moreover, the growing electronics industry and the need for advanced thermal management materials are augmenting the demand for Aluminum FRP.
