<p><p>Ultra High Purity Gas Purifiers consist of two main types: Point of Use Gas Purifiers and Bulk Gas Purifiers. Point of Use Gas Purifiers are compact and portable filtration systems that are designed to purify gas at the point where it is being used. They are commonly used in applications where a small volume of gas needs to be purified. On the other hand, Bulk Gas Purifiers are larger filtration systems that can handle a higher flow rate of gas. They are typically used in industries that require a larger volume of purified gas.</p></p>
