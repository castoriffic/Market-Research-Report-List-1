<p><p>Aquaculture feed market types include mash feed, pellet feed, and expanded feed. Mash feed refers to finely ground feed ingredients mixed into a uniform powder and fed directly to aquatic animals. Pellet feed involves grinding raw materials into a fine powder, adding binding agents, and shaping them into small pellets. Expanded feed involves a process of extrusion, where feed ingredients are passed through a machine that applies heat and pressure, resulting in a floating or sinking expanded pellet. These different feed types offer varying benefits and suitability for different species and growth stages of aquatic animals.</p></p>
