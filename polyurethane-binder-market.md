<p><p>The Polyurethane Binder Market is divided into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I refers to the polyurethane binders that are used primarily for coating applications, such as paints and varnishes. These binders are designed to provide cohesive and adhesive properties to the coatings, enhancing their durability and performance. On the other hand, Type II polyurethane binders are mostly used in adhesive applications, where they serve as a bonding agent for various materials. This type of binder ensures strong adhesion and enables the joining of different surfaces.</p></p>
