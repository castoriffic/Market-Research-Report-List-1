<p><p>The hydro-cylinder market consists of two main types: single acting and double-acting cylinders. Single-acting cylinders operate with hydraulic pressure in one direction, using either gravity or a spring to return to their original position. They are commonly used in applications such as lifting and pushing objects. On the other hand, double-acting cylinders use hydraulic pressure in both directions, allowing for precise control over movement. They find wide application in industries such as construction and manufacturing, where precise control and versatility are required.</p></p>
