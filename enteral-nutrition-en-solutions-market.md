<p><strong>The Enteral Nutrition (EN) Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Naso Gastric (NG) Feeding</li><li>Naso Duodenal Feeding</li><li>Naso Jejunal (NJ) Feeding</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enteral Nutrition (EN) Solutions market types refer to different methods of administering nutrition through the digestive tract. Naso Gastric (NG) Feeding involves delivering the solution through a tube that goes into the stomach through the nose. Naso Duodenal Feeding delivers the solution into the duodenum, the first part of the small intestine. Naso Jejunal (NJ) Feeding involves delivering the solution into the jejunum, the second part of the small intestine. "Others" refers to alternative methods, such as gastrostomy or jejunostomy, where the tube is inserted directly into the stomach or jejunum through a surgical opening.</p></p>
