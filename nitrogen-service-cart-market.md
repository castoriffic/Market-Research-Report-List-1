<p><p>A nitrogen service cart is a mobile equipment used in various industries for purging, pressure testing, leak testing, and servicing nitrogen systems. Firstly, the growing demand for nitrogen as an inert gas in various industries is contributing to the market's expansion. Nitrogen is widely used for its non-reactive properties in preventing oxidation and improving safety during various processes. This has led to an increased need for mobile and efficient nitrogen service carts.</p><p>Secondly, the rising focus on aviation safety and maintenance is driving the growth of the nitrogen service cart market. Aircraft maintenance requires regular nitrogen servicing for fuel tank inerting, tire inflation, and testing. As the global aviation industry continues to thrive, the demand for nitrogen service carts is expected to witness significant growth.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in nitrogen service cart technology, such as enhanced mobility, better operational efficiency, and increased capacity, are also fueling the market's growth.
