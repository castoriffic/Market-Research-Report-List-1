<p><p>Anisotropic Conductive Adhesive Film (ACF) is a type of adhesive film used in electronic manufacturing for connecting components. The market includes different types of ACFs such as Double Layer ACF and Three Layers ACF. Double Layer ACF consists of two layers of adhesive film with conductive particles in between, providing conductivity in one direction. Three Layers ACF, on the other hand, has an additional insulating layer between the two layers of adhesive film, allowing for conductivity in two perpendicular directions. These variations cater to different electronic assembly requirements.</p></p>
