<p><p>The invasive blood glucose meter market is segmented into various types based on the technology used to measure glucose levels. These types include GDH-FAD, GOD, Electrochemistry, GDH, and Other. GDH-FAD refers to the glucose dehydrogenase flavin adenine dinucleotide technology, which measures glucose levels using an enzyme. GOD stands for glucose oxidase technology, which also utilizes an enzyme to measure glucose. Electrochemistry involves using an electric current to detect glucose levels. GDH refers to glucose dehydrogenase technology, while Other represents other types of technologies used in invasive blood glucose meters.</p></p>
