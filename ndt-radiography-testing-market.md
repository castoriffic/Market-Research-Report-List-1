<p><strong>The NDT-Radiography Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Film Radiography</li><li>Real Time Radiography (RTR)</li><li>Computed Tomography (CT)</li><li>Digital Radiography (DR)</li><li>Computed Radiography (CR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NDT-Radiography Testing Market is segmented into several types. Film Radiography uses X-rays on film to detect defects. Real Time Radiography (RTR) captures real-time images using a continuous X-ray source, allowing for immediate evaluation. Computed Tomography (CT) offers detailed 3D images by combining multiple X-ray projections. Digital Radiography (DR) replaces traditional film with digital detectors, enabling faster results and easier storage. Computed Radiography (CR) uses reusable imaging plates, providing digital images by scanning the plate. These different methods offer various advantages for non-destructive testing of materials.</p></p>
