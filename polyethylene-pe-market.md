<p><p>Polyethylene (PE) is a widely used thermoplastic polymer with various market types. High-density polyethylene (HDPE) is known for its high strength and rigidity, making it suitable for applications like pipes, bottles, and rigid containers. Low-density polyethylene (LDPE) is more flexible and has good impact resistance, making it useful for applications such as films, packaging materials, and squeeze bottles. Linear low-density polyethylene (LLDPE) combines the properties of HDPE and LDPE, providing enhanced strength, flexibility, and impact resistance, making it suitable for applications like films, liners, and cables.</p></p>
