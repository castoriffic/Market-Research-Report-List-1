<p><p>The exposed framing glass curtain wall market comprises three major types, namely single glazed, double glazed, and three glazed types. Single glazed type is a basic option with a single layer of glass, offering minimal insulation and sound protection. Double glazed type consists of two glass layers separated by an air or gas-filled space, providing improved insulation and noise reduction. Three glazed type includes three glass layers with two insulating spaces, offering the highest level of insulation and soundproofing. These different types cater to diverse requirements in terms of energy efficiency and acoustic performance.</p></p>
