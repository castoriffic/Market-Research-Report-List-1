<p><p>The data fabric software market includes two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based data fabric software is hosted and accessed over the internet, enabling businesses to store and analyze data in virtual servers. It offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, on-premises data fabric software is installed locally on a company's own servers, providing businesses with full control and security over their data. Although it requires a higher upfront investment, it ensures data sovereignty. Organizations choose between these options based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
