<p><ul><li>Solution Types</li><li>Emulsion Types</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Styrene-Butadiene Rubber (SBR) based adhesive market can be categorized into two types: solution types and emulsion types. Solution types of SBR adhesives are liquid-based, where the rubber is dissolved in a solvent. These adhesives offer strong bonding properties and are commonly used in applications like laminating, packaging, and footwear. Emulsion types, on the other hand, are water-based adhesives where the SBR rubber is dispersed in water. These adhesives are known for their environmental friendliness, easy application, and lower cost, making them suitable for industries like woodworking and paperboard manufacturing.</p></p>
