<p><p>Tungsten Ribbons are available in two primary market types: Alloy and Pure Metal. Alloy Tungsten Ribbons are a blend of tungsten with other metals, resulting in enhanced properties such as improved strength, hardness, and resistance to corrosion. These ribbons are widely used in various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and electronics. On the other hand, Pure Metal Tungsten Ribbons consist solely of tungsten, offering the highest level of purity and excellent thermal and electrical conductivity. These ribbons find applications in electric lamps, electron microscopes, and X-ray tubes, among others.</p></p>
