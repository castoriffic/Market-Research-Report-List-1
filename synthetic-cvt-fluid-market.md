<p><strong>The Synthetic CVT Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fully Synthetic Oil</li><li>Semi Synthetic Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Synthetic CVT fluids are designed for continuous variable transmission (CVT) systems in vehicles. The market includes two types: fully synthetic and semi-synthetic oil. Fully synthetic oil is made entirely from synthetic base oils, offering superior performance and protection for CVT systems. It provides excellent lubrication, thermal stability, and resistance to oxidation and sludge formation. Semi-synthetic oil, on the other hand, is a blend of synthetic and conventional base oils. It combines some of the benefits of fully synthetic oil with the affordability of conventional oil, making it a popular choice for many vehicle owners.</p></p>
