<p><p>The rubber bearings market consists of various types of bearings made from rubber materials. Natural rubber bearings are made from raw rubber and provide good resilience and low damping characteristics. Lead rubber bearings use rubber pads bonded to a steel core, offering high damping and energy dissipation capabilities. High damping rubber bearings have enhanced vibration isolation properties. Other types of rubber bearings include elastomeric bearings, which provide flexibility and durability. These various types of rubber bearings cater to different industrial applications and requirements.</p></p>
