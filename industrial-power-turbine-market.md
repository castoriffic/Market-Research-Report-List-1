<p><p>The industrial power turbine market is categorized into two types: direct drive and indirect drive. Direct drive turbines are connected directly to the load or equipment they power, eliminating the need for any mechanical transmission system. On the other hand, indirect drive turbines consist of a mechanical transmission system, such as a gearbox, that is used to connect the turbine to the load. The choice between these two types depends on factors such as the desired power output, efficiency, and cost-effectiveness for specific industrial applications.</p></p>
