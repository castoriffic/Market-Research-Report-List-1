These technologies enable baggage handling systems to perform intelligent operations, optimize routes, and identify potential issues or anomalies, thereby enhancing efficiency and reducing operational costs.</p><p>Another trend involves the adoption of automation and robotics in baggage handling systems. Automated sorting and scanning systems, along with robotic arms for loading and unloading baggage, are becoming increasingly prevalent in airports worldwide.
