<p><strong>The Large size TFT LCD Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TN</li><li>IPS</li><li>AFFS</li><li>MVA</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The large-size TFT LCD market is comprised of various types, including TN, IPS, AFFS, MVA, and others. TN (Twisted Nematic) panels offer fast response times and low production costs, but suffer from limited viewing angles and color accuracy. IPS (In-Plane Switching) panels provide superior color reproduction and wider viewing angles, yet are more expensive to produce. AFFS (Advanced Fringe Field Switching) panels offer enhanced image quality and reduced power consumption. MVA (Multi-Domain Vertical Alignment) panels deliver high contrast ratios and good color reproduction. Other types include PLS (Plane to Line Switching) and VA (Vertical Alignment) panels, each with their own unique features and advantages.</p></p>
