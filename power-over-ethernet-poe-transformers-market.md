<p><p>The Power over Ethernet (PoE) Transformers market includes two main types: Flyback Type and Forward Type. Flyback Type transformers are commonly used in low-power applications, offering smaller size, simplified design, and lower cost. They provide galvanic isolation and are suitable for applications like IP phones and wireless access points. On the other hand, Forward Type transformers are used in high-power applications and offer higher efficiency and power density. They are ideal for devices such as IP cameras and industrial automation equipment. Both types cater to different power requirements and have their own unique advantages.</p></p>
