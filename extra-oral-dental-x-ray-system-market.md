<p><p>The Extra Oral Dental X-Ray System market consists of two main types: Panoramic/Cephalometric X-Ray Units and Cone-Beamed Computed Tomography (CBCT) Systems. Panoramic/Cephalometric X-Ray Units take a broad view of the entire oral cavity, capturing the jawbones, teeth, and surrounding structures. These provide a comprehensive assessment of the dental health. On the other hand, CBCT Systems capture three-dimensional images of the oral and maxillofacial region, offering a more detailed evaluation of bone structure, tooth positioning, and impacted teeth. Both types of X-Ray systems play a crucial role in diagnosing dental conditions and planning treatment.</p></p>
