<p><p>The thermal spray products market consists of three main types: thermal spray wires, thermal spray powders, and others. Thermal spray wires are solid metal or alloy wires used in the thermal spray process to create a protective coating on surfaces. Thermal spray powders are fine particles of metal, ceramic, or alloy materials that are heated and propelled onto a surface to form a coating. The 'others' market segment includes various types of thermal spray products such as rods, cords, and tapes, which are used for specific coating applications.</p></p>
