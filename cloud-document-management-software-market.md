<p><p>Cloud document management software market can be categorized into three types: Public Cloud, Private Cloud, and Hybrid Cloud. Public Cloud refers to a service where the software is hosted on the vendor's infrastructure and accessible to multiple users over the internet. Private Cloud, on the other hand, involves the software being deployed on a private network, ensuring enhanced security and control for the organization. Hybrid Cloud combines both public and private clouds, allowing organizations to optimize their resources by storing sensitive data on private and non-sensitive data on public clouds.</p></p>
