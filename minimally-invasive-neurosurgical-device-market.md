<p><p>The minimally invasive neurosurgical device market can be categorized into three types: surgical devices, monitoring and visualization devices, and endoscopy devices. Monitoring and visualization devices help neurosurgeons to assess brain activity and monitor vital signs during surgery. Endoscopy devices allow for the insertion of a tiny camera and surgical tools into the body, enabling surgeons to visualize and operate on targeted areas with minimal incisions. These devices aim to improve patient outcomes and shorten recovery time.</p></p>
