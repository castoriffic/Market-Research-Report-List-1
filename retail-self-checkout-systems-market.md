<p><p>The retail self-checkout systems market consists of three main types: stand-alone self-checkout systems, wall-mounted self-checkout systems, and countertop self-checkout systems. Stand-alone systems are free-standing units that customers can use to scan and pay for their items. Wall-mounted systems are fixed to the wall in designated areas, providing a more space-efficient option. Countertop systems are smaller and designed to sit on existing store counters. These different types offer retailers flexibility in choosing the most suitable self-checkout solution based on their store layout and customer needs.</p></p>
