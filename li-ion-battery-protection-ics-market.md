<p><p>The Li-ion battery protection ICs market is divided into two types: single-cell and multi-cell. Single-cell protection ICs are designed to ensure the safety and efficient performance of a single Li-ion battery cell. They provide functions such as overcharge protection, over-discharge protection, and short circuit protection. On the other hand, multi-cell protection ICs are specifically designed for battery packs with multiple Li-ion cells connected in series or parallel. They offer additional features like cell balancing, temperature monitoring, and communication interfaces to manage the overall battery pack performance.</p></p>
