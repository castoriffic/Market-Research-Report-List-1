<p><strong>The Automotive Hydraulic Power Steering Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Hydraulic Power</li><li>Constant Flow Hydraulic Power</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive hydraulic power steering systems market consists of two main types: pressure hydraulic power and constant flow hydraulic power. In pressure hydraulic power systems, a conventional hydraulic pump generates pressure and pushes hydraulic fluid to assist in steering. On the other hand, constant flow hydraulic power systems use a hydraulic pump that provides a continuous flow of hydraulic fluid, ensuring a consistent amount of power assistance for steering. Both types contribute to improved steering control and maneuverability in vehicles.</p></p>
