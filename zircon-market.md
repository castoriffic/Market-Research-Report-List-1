<p><strong>The Zircon Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magmatic Zircon</li><li>Hydrothermal Zircon</li><li>Metamorphic Zircon</li><li>Detrital Zircon</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Zircon is found in various geological settings, leading to different market types. Magmatic zircon refers to zircon formed during volcanic or plutonic activities, and is known for its high crystallinity and stability. Hydrothermal zircon forms in hydrothermal veins and is valued for its high purity. Metamorphic zircon is created through intense heat and pressure during geological transformations. Detrital zircon is derived from the erosion and deposition of rocks, making it an important tool for dating geological events. These different market types reflect the diverse sources and uses of zircon in various industries.</p></p>
