<p><ul><li>Analog Vibration Meter</li><li>Digital Vibration Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vibration meter market can be classified into two main types: analog vibration meters and digital vibration meters. Analog vibration meters employ a mechanical system to measure vibrations and display the results using an analog dial or needle. They are simple to use but may lack advanced features. On the other hand, digital vibration meters utilize electronic sensors to measure vibrations and display the results on a digital screen. They offer more accuracy, versatility, and advanced functionalities compared to analog meters.</p></p>
