<p><p>The Artificial Wood Panel Market encompasses various types of panels used in construction and furniture manufacturing. Particleboard (PB) is made from wood particles bonded with resin, while Medium Density Fibreboard (MDF) is composed of wood fibers mixed with resin and compressed. High Density Fibreboard (HDF) is similar to MDF but denser. Oriented Strand Board (OSB) is constructed using layers of wood strands glued together. Plywood is made from thin veneers of wood stacked and glued together. Other types may include Laminated Veneer Lumber (LVL) and Hardboard. All these products offer different characteristics and applications in the industry.</p></p>
