<p><p>X-Ray stretchers are used to transport patients safely during X-Ray procedures. There are three main types of X-Ray stretchers in the market. Pneumatic stretchers use air pressure to elevate and lower the stretcher, offering easy maneuverability. Hydraulic stretchers use fluid pressure to adjust the height, providing smooth movements. Electrical stretchers are operated by electricity and typically offer more advanced features such as adjustable positions and integrated monitoring systems. These different types cater to the varying needs and preferences of healthcare facilities and professionals.</p></p>
